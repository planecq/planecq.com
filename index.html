About

Thibaut Tiberghien

Founder & Director (PhD)

After years of research on Intelligent Systems, it is clear that while technology is moving rapidly, solutions are built for corporations and cities, products are created for the consumer electronics market, but smaller companies are left out. Mass market products are usually not designed with their usecase in mind, and corporate solutions are often too complex and inaccessible. Planecq was incorporated to try and fill this gap. We hope to be an accessible source of technological solutions. One that you will find approachable in communication, agile in implementation, and reliable in delivering the systems you need. Our philosophy is to build smart systems that are tailored to your needs, using the best technologies available, yet keeping them as simple to use as possible. We hope to become the technical partner of your business, one that you can count on to understand your operations, to build, deploy and maintain solutions for you, providing support and training when necessary. We are a small company too, let's grow together.
— Thibaut Tiberghien, 2016.

Holder of a double degree from France and Germany in the fields of Electrical Engineering, Information Technology and Human-Machine Interaction, Thibaut moved to Singapore to further study Computer Science and get his PhD. During his Doctorate and Post-doctoral years as a Research Fellow, he specialized in the field of Ambient Intelligence, building Context-Aware Systems for Smart Homes, and Smart Cities. He is passionate about Connected Objects, the Internet of Things, and Tangible Interfaces; and battles to make these technologies accessible by developing user-centered web-based solutions to build smarter spaces.
