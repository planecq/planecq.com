Services

We provide Development, Consulting and Research services, with an expertise in the areas of Web Applications, Internet of Things (IoT) and Smart Spaces. Whether you need a cloud-based process management app for your business, you want to prototype a connected object for your industry, or you plan to connect you production line with sensors to build a smart factory; we can provide services and solutions customized to your needs. You can run the application we build locally, or we can deploy it on our cloud computing platform hosted in the best datacenters, ensuring realiability and scalability. We can take care of deployment, support and maintenance on the solution we build for you.
